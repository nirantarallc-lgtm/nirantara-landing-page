1. What NourishIQ Does

NourishIQ is a food ingredient analysis tool. It helps you understand what’s in packaged food and FMCG products by:

  • Scanning product barcodes to identify products
  • Looking up ingredients against a curated database of food additives and their regulatory status across countries
  • Displaying signals and explanations about individual ingredients
  • Generating AI-powered insights to help you understand ingredient information in context
In simple terms: We surface ingredient information and regulatory data to help you make informed choices. We do not tell you what to eat or not eat.
