Delete Your NourishIQ Account

Effective Date: March 30, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your NourishIQ account and how associated data is handled after your request. It is intended to help users clearly understand the deletion process and related retention obligations.

You can request account deletion directly inside the NourishIQ app or by emailing support@nourishiq.com.

1. How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion using either of the following methods:

In the App

  1. Open the NourishIQ app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions.

By Email

  • Email: support@nourishiq.com
  • Subject: “Account Deletion Request”

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account information (name, email, profile)
  • App usage data linked to your account
  • Preferences and saved data

3. What Data May Be Retained

  • Transaction records retained for legal and accounting purposes
  • Anonymous, non-identifiable analytics data

4. Retention Timeline

Account and personal data will be deleted within 7 days of receiving your deletion request.

5. Contact

If you need help with an account deletion request, contact us at support@nourishiq.com.
