1 Who We Are

Ashirvad Daily Blessings is an independent mobile app that delivers personalised Hindu deity blessings, mantras, and daily prayers.

Developer: Nirantara
Website: nirantara.online
Contact: hello@nirantara.online

2 Data Stored on Your Device

All of the following is stored only on your device using Android's local storage. It is never uploaded to any server and never shared with any third party.

  • Your chosen deity (e.g. Ganesh, Shiva, Lakshmi)
  • Your preferred prayer music selection
  • Your display name (if entered)
  • Your daily prayer streak and prayer history
  • Your total prayer time
  • Your preferred daily notification time
You can erase all of this data at any time via:
Android Settings → Apps → Ashirvad → Storage → Clear Data

3 Network Connections

The app makes one type of network request — fetching daily blessing content from our content database. This request contains only:

  • The name of your selected deity
  • Today's date

No device identifiers, no personal data, and no account information are ever sent. All connections use HTTPS (encrypted in transit).

4 Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules a daily local reminder at your chosen time. This is processed entirely on your device — no notification content or schedule is transmitted to any server.

You can disable notifications at any time via:
Android Settings → Apps → Ashirvad → Notifications

5 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking of any kind
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

6 Third-Party Services

The app uses one third-party service:

Supabase (supabase.com)
Purpose: Hosting daily blessing content — read-only database
Data sent: Selected deity name + today's date only
Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising platforms, or crash reporting services are used.

7 Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
