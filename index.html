Who We Are

Ashirvad – Daily Blessings is a mobile application developed by Nirantara LLP. The app provides personalised Hindu deity blessings, mantra chanting, and daily spiritual guidance.

Developer: Nirantara LLP
Website: https://nirantara.online
Support: ashirvad.support@nirantara.online

Data Stored on Your Device

Ashirvad is designed with a privacy-first approach. Most information is stored locally on your mobile device using the device’s local storage.

This may include:

  • Your selected deity (e.g. Ganesh, Shiva, Lakshmi)
  • Your preferred prayer music
  • Your display name (if entered)
  • Your daily prayer streak and prayer history
  • Your total prayer time
  • Your preferred reminder time
  • The list of apps selected for Sankalp Mode blocking
  • Sankalp countdown progress if a session is interrupted

This information remains stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Retention

All preferences and prayer activity remain stored locally on your device until you uninstall the application or manually clear the application storage through your device settings.

Sankalp Mode

Ashirvad includes an optional feature called Sankalp Mode. This feature helps users build discipline by requiring a short prayer or countdown before opening selected apps.

When enabled, the user can select specific applications that should trigger Sankalp Mode.

To enable this functionality, the app may request permission to access application usage information on the device. This allows the app to detect when a selected application is opened.

The list of selected apps and Sankalp session progress are stored locally on the device and are never transmitted to external servers.

Network Connections

The application connects to backend services only to retrieve daily blessing content and related media.

These requests may include:

  • The selected deity name
  • The current date

The app may also load deity images from image hosting services (including Supabase Storage or other image hosting providers). These requests are used only to display images within the app.

No personal identifiers or user information are transmitted with these requests.

All connections are secured using HTTPS encryption.

App Permissions

Ashirvad may request certain device permissions to enable specific features of the application.

  • Internet – used to retrieve daily blessing content and images
  • Notifications – used to send optional daily prayer reminders
  • Usage Access – used by Sankalp Mode to detect when selected apps are opened
  • Boot Completed – used to restore scheduled reminders after the device restarts

These permissions are used only to enable app functionality and are not used to collect personal information.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules a daily reminder locally on your device.

Notification scheduling happens entirely on your device and no reminder data is transmitted externally.

Notifications can be disabled at any time via your device’s notification settings.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • No location tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No user accounts
  • No sale or sharing of personal data

Children’s Privacy

Ashirvad is suitable for users of all ages. The application does not collect personal information from any users, including children under the age of 13.

All user preferences, prayer activity, and settings are stored locally on the user's device and are not transmitted to external servers.

If you have questions about privacy or data practices in the Ashirvad app, please contact us at ashirvad.support@nirantara.online.
